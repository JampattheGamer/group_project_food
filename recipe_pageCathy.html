Why beef stew? Simple, beef stew is a relatively easy recipe that allows for a large amount of variation and can be stored easily. I am gonna go through a recipe here but keep in mind that these instructions are not set in stone and if you feel you could make the stew better, feel free to try your hand at your own additions.

The ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 2 lb. beef chuck stew meat, cubed into 1" pieces
  • 1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and cut into rounds
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 c. tomato paste
  • 6 c. low-sodium beef broth
  • 1 c. red wine
  • 1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp. dried or fresh thyme leaves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 lb. baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 c. frozen peas
  • 1/4 c. freshly chopped parsley, for garnish
